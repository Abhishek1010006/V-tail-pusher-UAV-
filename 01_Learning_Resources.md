# 01 — Learning Resources

> *"It is possible to fly without motors, but not without knowledge and skill."*
> — Wilbur Wright

## Overview

This document contains the primary learning resources used during the design and development of the **V-Tail Pusher UAV**. These references were selected to build a strong foundation in fixed-wing aircraft design, aerodynamics, airfoil selection, structural design, and UAV development.

The resources include university lecture series, practical build tutorials, and standard aerospace reference books that guided various stages of this project.

---

## Learning Objectives

These resources were studied to understand:

- Fundamentals of fixed-wing aircraft design
- Aerodynamics and lift generation
- Airfoil selection and wing design
- V-tail aircraft configuration
- Flight stability and control
- UAV structural design principles
- Practical aircraft construction techniques

---

## Recommended Learning Path

For new contributors, it is recommended to follow the resources in the following order:

1. NPTEL – Design of Fixed Wing UAVs
2. Introduction to Flight (Chapter 5)
3. Experimental Airlines – V-Tail Aircraft Build

Following this sequence provides both the theoretical background and practical understanding required for the project.
